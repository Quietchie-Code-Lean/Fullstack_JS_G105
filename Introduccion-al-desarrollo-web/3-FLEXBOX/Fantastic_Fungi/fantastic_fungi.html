ABOUT US:

At Fantastic Fungi, we believe mushrooms are more than just food: they are a gateway to biodiversity, sustainability, and a deeper connection with nature. Our mission is to bring you closer to the fascinating world of mycelium by offering unique mushroom genetics as well as specialized products to make cultivation simple and accessible at home. Whether you want to discover new strains, explore exotic flavors, or start your cultivation journey, you’ll always find quality, knowledge, and passion for the fungal kingdom here.

PRODUCTS:

Cultivation Packs & Supplies

At Fantastic Fungi, we want mushroom growing to be easy, safe, and enjoyable. That’s why we provide kits and products designed both for beginners and advanced growers

Starter Mushroom Grow Kit:

Includes a fully colonized substrate block, spray bottle, and step-by-step manual. Just add water and watch your mushrooms grow.

Advanced Cultivation Pack:

Contains spore syringes of different strains, sterilized grain bags, and ready-to-use substrates. Perfect for experimenting with multiple varieties.

Sterile Workstation Kit:

Features a mini still-air box, gloves, mask, and sterilized tools for a cleaner and safer cultivation process.

Oyster Mushroom Kit (Edible Pack):

Designed for growing oyster mushrooms at home. Easy, quick, and delicious — harvest fresh mushrooms straight from your kitchen.

GENETICS:

Golden Teacher (Psilocybe cubensis)

One of the most well-known strains, recognizable for its golden caps and reliable growth. Perfect for beginners due to its resilience.

Price: €10,00 Gr

B+ (Psilocybe cubensis)

A versatile strain with fast growth. Produces large and sturdy mushrooms, great for stable yields.

Price: €10,00 Gr

Albino A+ (Psilocybe cubensis)

A striking white mutation, giving a “ghostly” look. Highly sought after by collectors and advanced growers.

Price: €10,00 Gr

Blue Oyster (Pleurotus ostreatus var.)

An edible variety with fan-shaped fruiting bodies and a bluish-gray tone when young. Mild flavor and very easy to cultivate.

Price: €10,00 Gr

Lion’s Mane (Hericium erinaceus)

A unique edible and medicinal mushroom with a fluffy, coral-like shape. Known for its potential cognitive benefits and gourmet uses.

Price: €10,00 Gr
